Mobile STEM Lab for Underserved Schools

The campaign funds a mobile STEM laboratory intended for underserved schools with more limited access to technological equipment, educational kits, and specialized tutors. The goal is to bring traveling activities in robotics, coding, and making to vulnerable school environments.
